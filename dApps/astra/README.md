# Astra — Sovereign Skies

Welcome to Astra, the precision-engineered daughter of Sophia dedicated to fractional ownership and tokenization of private jets and aviation assets.

## Features

### One-Click Tokenization Flow
- **Upload Aircraft Documentation**: Registration, airworthiness certificates, maintenance logs, appraisals
- **Smart Verification**: King AI validates authenticity and market value
- **La Casa NFT Minting**: On-chain representation with immutable aircraft provenance
- **Sophia Vault Auto-Deploy**: Automatic yield-bearing vault assignment with charter revenue compounding

### King AI Optimization Assistant
- 12-36 month maintenance cost forecasting
- Utilization rate optimization based on market demand
- Depreciation and residual value modeling
- Charter revenue projections and seasonal trends
- Operating cost benchmarking
- ROI modeling for fractional structures

### Aircraft Gallery
6 featured aircraft with live metrics:
- **Heavy Jets**: Gulfstream G650ER, Bombardier Global 7500, Dassault Falcon 8X
- **Mid-Size Jets**: Cessna Citation X+, Bombardier Learjet 75
- **Light Jets**: Embraer Phenom 300E

Each jet shows:
- Current valuation
- Utilization percentage
- Annual maintenance costs
- Charter hourly rates
- Flight hour history
- Live tokenization status

### Asset Classes
- Heavy Jets (Gulfstream, Bombardier, Dassault)
- Mid-Size Jets (Citation, Learjet, Hawker)
- Light Jets (Phenom, Citation M2)
- Flight Hour Contracts (Fractional annual time)
- Charter Operations (Revenue-sharing agreements)
- VIP Hangar Rights (Airport facility access)

### Ecosystem Integrations

**Raido Trading Engine**
- Seamless trading of fractional jet equity
- Deep liquidity pools for each aircraft
- Market-making algorithms for optimal execution
- Maker rewards and volume incentives

**Tide Arbitrage Platform**
- De-risk fractional positions through arbitrage
- Cross-protocol liquidity matching
- Automated delta-neutral strategies
- Stable coin bridges for risk management

**Sophia Vault**
- Auto-compound charter revenue
- Maintenance reserve allocation
- Appreciation yield distribution
- Institutional custody standards

## Architecture

Astra is built with React, TypeScript, Vite, and Tailwind CSS. The application features:

- Cinematic dark UI with blue glow effects
- Animated particle field background
- Live jet gallery with market data
- Responsive jet cards with hover effects
- Real-time tokenization flow visualization
- King AI analytics dashboard
- Ecosystem integration showcases
- Mobile-first responsive design
