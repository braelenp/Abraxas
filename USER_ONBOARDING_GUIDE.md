## Step 1: Get ABRA Tokens

ABRA is the access token for Abraxas. You need to hold some to access the dApp and mint NFTs.

### Option A: Swap for ABRA (If you have SOL/USDC)
1. In Abraxas, click **"Trade"** tab (ᛚ Laguz)
2. Click **"Swap"** tab
3. Use Jupiter DEX to swap your SOL/USDC → ABRA
4. Sign with Phantom wallet
5. Wait ~5 seconds for transaction to settle

### Option B: Buy ABRA 0% Fees (Recommended)
1. In Abraxas, click **"Trade"** tab
2. Click **"Bags (0% Fee)"** tab
3. Enter amount you want to buy
4. Sign with Phantom wallet
5. Tokens arrive instantly

**Result**: You now have ABRA tokens in your Phantom wallet.

## Step 2: Mint a La Casa NFT (Asset Ownership Record)

This NFT represents your real-world asset on-chain. Could be real estate, music rights, art, gaming equity, etc.

1. Click **"Tokenize"** (in navigation)
2. Fill out the asset details:
   - Asset name (e.g., "Brooklyn Brownstone")
   - Collection type (real estate, music rights, etc.)
   - Estimated value
3. Click **"Mint La Casa NFT"** button
4. Sign the Phantom transaction
5. Your NFT appears in your Phantom wallet + in Abraxas dashboard

**Cost**: Some ABRA tokens (exact amount TBD) - this is the minting fee
**Result**: You now own an on-chain record of your asset ownership.

## Step 3: Create an AI-Managed Vault

Vaults are where your NFTs live and get managed.

1. Click **"My Vaults"** (in navigation)
2. Click **"Create Your First Vault"**
3. Enter vault name (e.g., "Real Estate Holdings")
4. Choose asset type (Real Estate, Music Rights, DApp Equity, etc.)
5. Click **"Create Vault"**

**Result**: Empty vault created and ready for assets.

---

## Step 4: Assign an AI Manager

Sophia AI agents manage your vault 24/7.

1. Look at your newly created vault
2. Click the vault
3. Under "Assign AI Manager," pick one:
   - **Sophia Sentinel** = Conservative (protects principal)
   - **Sophia Yield** = Growth (balanced growth)
   - **Sophia Defensive** = Risk-managed (minimizes volatility)
4. Click to assign

**Result**: AI agent is now managing your vault.

---

## Step 5: Deposit Your NFT

Move your La Casa NFT from your wallet into the vault for management.

1. In your vault details, click **"Deposit NFT"**
2. Select the La Casa NFT you want to deposit
3. Confirm the transaction in Phantom
4. Wait for settlement (~5 seconds)

**Result**: Your NFT is now in the vault, and the AI agent begins managing it.
